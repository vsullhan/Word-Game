Help Page
In order to start a game, you need to click one of these buttons:
There are two single player modes. In the first mode, you have ten rounds to guess a word from a given substring in as little time as possible, the less time you take to get a word, the more points you get. In the second mode, you keep playing until you are unable to guess a word. Once you fail to guess a word in the given amount of time, the game ends.
After you click one of the above buttons, you will be asked to select a difficulty. In Easy mode, you get 10 seconds to guess a word containing a two letter long substring. In Medium mode, you are given 7.5 seconds to guess a word containing a three letter long substring. In Hard mode, you get 5 seconds to guess a word containing a four letter long substring
Once you start the game, you will see the amount of time you have remaining, your current score/round, and the substring, as well as this:
Type your guess into the input section and hit the enter button on your keyboard Once you have your guess. If your guess is not a word, you will be told so and the timer will keep going down.
Once the game is over, you will be directed to the game over page, from which you can get back to the home page.
